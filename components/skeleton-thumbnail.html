# Skeleton thumbnail

Skeleton thumbnail is used to provide a low fidelity representation of an image before it appears on the page, and improves load times perceived by users. Use for thumbnail in or outside of a card.

# Medium thumbnail

Use this component to represent medium thumbnails.

# Large thumbnail
